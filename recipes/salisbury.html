Salisbury Steak

Salisbury steak with a savory onion sauce. This yummy recipe has been in my family for years. It's easy to cook but tastes like it took hours to make! I usually make enough extra sauce to pour over mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Canned Soup
  • Seasonings & Sauces

Steps

  1. Make and shape the patties.
  2. Brown the patties on both sides, then drain the excess fat.
  3. Make and season the gravy.
  4. Pour the gravy over the patties and continue cooking until the meat is cooked through.
